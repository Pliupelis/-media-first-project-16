Our company

Since 1955, Statandva Company has been setting the industry standard for performance and technical innovation in geotechnical construction. As the North American subsidiary of Soletanche Bachy, Statandva is part of a global network of unparalleled geotechnical resources and expertise, specializing in deep foundations, earth retention, ground treatment and ground improvement. We have built our reputation by performing the highest quality work and providing the most innovative solutions on the widest range of demanding and high profile projects. With district offices around the country Statandva has the capabilities and local expertise to address each region’s unique geotechnical construction needs. For over 50 years, Statandva has pioneered U.S. geotechnical construction – developing and introducing new techniques and completing many technically challenging, high-profile projects.
